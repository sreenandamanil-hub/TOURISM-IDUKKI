Welcome to Idukki Tourism

Welcome to Idukki, one of the most beautiful hill districts in Kerala. Known for its green mountains, cool climate, waterfalls, rivers and wildlife, Idukki is a paradise for nature lovers.

Popular destinations include Munnar, Ramakkalmedu, Vagamon, Thekkady, Idukki Dam, tea plantations, spice gardens and wildlife sanctuaries.

Idukki is also rich in history and culture. Visitors can enjoy trekking, boating, wildlife watching and peaceful landscapes throughout the district.
